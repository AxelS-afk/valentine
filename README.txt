VALENTINE'S DAY GIFT WEBSITE
============================

Congratulations on this beautiful gift! Here's how to personalize the website with your photos.

INCLUDED FILES
--------------
- index.html : The main website page
- styles.css : All styles and animations
- script.js : Interactions and transitions

HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
-----------------------

The website has 4 spots for your photos:

1. COUPLE PHOTO (home page)
   - Filename: photo-couple.jpg
   - Recommended format: Square (e.g. 800x800px)
   - Description: A beautiful photo of you two together

2. GIFT 1 PHOTO (Flowers and chocolates)
   - Filename: gift1-photo.jpg
   - Recommended format: Landscape (e.g. 800x600px)
   - Description: Photo of flowers or chocolates (optional)

3. GIFT 2 PHOTO (Golden heart)
   - Filename: gift2-photo.jpg
   - Recommended format: Landscape (e.g. 800x600px)
   - Description: Romantic photo of you two (optional)

4. GIFT 3 PHOTO (Love coupons)
   - This section doesn't have a photo, it displays the coupon list

STEPS TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
-------------------------

1. Prepare your photos:
   - Rename them exactly as indicated above
   - Accepted formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png
   - Make sure photos aren't too large (max 2MB each)

2. Place your photos:
   - Put all files (index.html, styles.css, script.js)
     and your photos in the SAME FOLDER

3. Test the website:
   - Double-click on index.html to open it in your browser
   - Check that all photos display correctly

ADDITIONAL CUSTOMIZATION
-------------------------

If you want to modify the texts or love coupons,
open the index.html file with a text editor and look for:

- For coupons: section with class="coupons-list"
- For messages: look for <h1>, <h2>, <p> tags

IMPORTANT NOTES
---------------

- Photos 1, 2 and 3 are optional. If you don't add them,
  a placeholder will display instead
- The couple photo (photo-couple.jpg) is highly recommended
  to personalize the home page
- The website is fully responsive and works on mobile,
  tablet and computer
- No internet connection is needed once the files are
  downloaded

ONLINE HOSTING (optional)
--------------------------

If you want to share the website via a link, you can host it
for free on:
- GitHub Pages
- Netlify
- Vercel

Just upload all files to a folder and follow
the instructions of your chosen platform.
